Titan is an indian product company

Titanic company commenced opperations in 1984 under the name of Titan Watches Limited. It launched its youth fashion accessories brand Fastrack.

The company is the largest branded jewellery maker in India. Titan watch company

More than 80% of its total revenues coming from the jewellery segment. As of 2022, Titan has a 6% market share in India's jewellery market.

As of 2019, it is also the fifth-largest watch manufacturer in the world. It is most trusted Indian Brand.

HISTORY

1984-1990

Titan Company Limited was inaugurated on 26 jully 1984 under the name Titan Watches Limited in Chennai.

A plant was set up to manufacture quartz analog electonic watches in the State Industries Promotion Corporation of Tamil Nadu, Ltd.

Industrial area at Hosur. In November 1986, a satellite case plant was set up in Dehradun, Uttarakhand at present with a maufacturing capacity of 500,000 watch cases annually.

1991-2000
  • In Septembe 1993, the company changed its name to Titan Industries Ltd. as it ventured into other range of products other than watches.
  • In 1994, Titan launched its jewellery brand Tanishq.
  • In 1998, the launched its watch & accessories brand, Fastrack, targeted at a younger audience in a bid to computer with Timex.
2001-2010
  • in 2001, Titan launched kids watches brand, Dash. The brand saw poor performance and was discontinued in 2003.
  • In 2004, the company entered into an agreement with Moet Hennessy Louis Vuitton Group,to service the latter's range of watches in India through its customer care centres.
  • In 2005, Fastrack was positioned as an independent accessory brand targeting the urban youth. With the objective to become a fashion brand, Fastrack launched Sunglasses in the same yeaar and launched bags, belts and wallets in 2009.
2011-Present
  1. Titan acquired Swiss watch maker Favre-Leuba in 2011 to enter the European market.
  2. In 2013, Titan entered the fragrances segment with the brand Skinn[23] and later that year, it ventured into the helmets category under its brand Fastrack.
  3. In the same year, it changed its name to Titan Company Ltd.[25] In 2014, it entered into a joint venture with Montblanc to establish its retail stores in India.
  4. In 2016, Titan opened prescription lens manufacturing facilities in Noida, Kolkata, and Mumbai to improve its order processing time.[27] In 2018, Titan merged its jewellery brand, Gold Plus targeted at customers in South India with Tanishq to establish the brand's presence in South.
  5. Taneira is an ethnic wear brand from Titan that retails hand-woven sarees from different weaving clusters of India.[29] The brand was launched in 2016. The first retail store was opened in Bengaluru in 2017 with more stores followed in New Delhi and Hyderabad.
